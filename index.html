About me

I am currently a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics, Texas A&M University, mentored by Tian Yang.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2019 at the Department of Mathematics University of California, Davis under the supervision of Greg Kuperberg.
I recieved my B.S. degree in both Economics and Mathematics in 2013 from the Wuhan University.

I am interested in Quantum algebra, especially its applications in Geometric topology, Representation theory and Group theory.
